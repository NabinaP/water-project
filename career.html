DISCOVER PROJECT

About

    History Culture Safety Whitewater Cares Locations Careers

Have a passion for waterparks and the amusement industry? We’re seeking creative individuals who are inspired to develop and support innovative fun play products. Come work with one of Canada’s Best Managed Companies!

We celebrate our diversity and highly value our employees. Our uniqueness is what makes us a creative, energetic, fast paced and innovative company. We are an exciting organization in a niche international industry that promotes a positive work environment. WhiteWater is led by a team of over 600 talented employees in 8 service offices and 15 sales locations around the world. Our headquarters is in Vancouver, BC.

Current Opportunities

WhiteWater has a wide spectrum of career opportunities which touches on all areas of a project and product life cycle. As a customer focused organization, we provide complete services to our clients from design, engineering, manufacturing, installation and after sales support. Our diverse career paths include Sales and Marketing, Design, Engineering, Project Management, Manufacturing, Supply Chain, Construction, and Support Services.

An exciting career at WhiteWater includes many exciting benefits including extended health and dental, RRSP matching, support for training and development, and a waterpark entrance fee allowance. Drive, transit or bike to work; WhiteWater provides secure biking facilities and onsite showers.
